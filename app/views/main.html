Welcome to MassData, the new Commonwealth open data website. Intended to be a "one-stop shop" that provides easy access to already-available, open data MassData highlights the Commonwealth's commitment to transparency in government.

LEARN

About open data and the MassData initiative

The Commonwealth has a lot of open data, but it is difficult to find and most people have no idea what is already available. MassData includes state-level data from a number of participating agencies, as well as municipal data from three cities: Boston, Somerville, and Cambridge.

PUTTING TECHNOLOGY TO WORK

The MassData initiative serves to increase public awareness of and access to government data, enhance government transparency and accountability, and encourage economic development and civic engagement.

UNLOCKING OPPORTUNITY

MassData is a place for research, visualization and the exploration of existing public data sets in the Commonwealth. By Providing easy, user-friendly access to open data is a conerstone of the Commonwealth's digital strategy for driving innovation.
By consolidating and publishing open state and local data, government can help technologists, businesses and residents develop new solutions that support and catalyze the work of state government.
